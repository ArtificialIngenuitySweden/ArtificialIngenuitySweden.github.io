Privacy Policy — Bergslagen AR

Last updated: September 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Bergslagen AR is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app operates with respect to personal data and the steps taken to minimise data collection.

2. Local Processing — No Personal Data Collected

Bergslagen AR processes all augmented reality and detection features locally on your device. The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal information, location data, or images captured by your device.

3. Mapbox

Bergslagen AR uses Mapbox for mapping and related map tiles. While the app itself does not send data to external servers, Mapbox may collect limited anonymous diagnostic or usage data according to Mapbox's policies. For details, please review Mapbox's privacy information.

Mapbox privacy details: https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy

4. No APIs or External Services

This version of Bergslagen AR does not use any external APIs or backend services for detection, configuration, or storage. All configuration and detection run locally on your device.

5. No Persistent Storage

The app does not save or transmit images, locations, or personal information to us or any third parties. Any temporary data used for live detection is kept only in memory and cleared when the app is closed or when the device reclaims resources.

6. Data Security

Because Bergslagen AR does not collect or transmit personal data, the risk of data breaches is minimized. We still follow secure development practices to reduce potential vulnerabilities in the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Bergslagen AR is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you suspect a child has shared personal information, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be made available inside the app. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: info@artificialingenuity.io
